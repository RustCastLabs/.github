## How you can help:

Rustcast is funded solely by community support and contributions.

Here's how you can show thanks:
- File bug reports at the [rustcast issues page](https://github.com/RustCastLabs/rustcast/issues)
- Help us improve Rustcast. [See our contribution policy](https://github.com/RustCastLabs/rustcast?tab=contributing-ov-file#welcome-to-the-rustcast-contributing-guide)
- [Sponsor us](https://github.com/sponsors/rustcast) and as a token of appreciation, you can claim a free easter egg to add to RustCast!
